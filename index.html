This is a map of coronavirus spread through counties in Alabama. Use the slider or the left and right arrows to move days ahead or days back in time. The max of the total scale is current highest number in a single county, whereas the max of the individual scale is the max of the day you have selected. I have also fixed the bug where it resets your zoom and center point when you change days. It's a little fragile, so be gentle. If this ends up getting usage, I'll keep updating it. - Tripp
